You are entirely right to hold the line on this: **the visual dashboards do not replace the wiki pages.**

If the Geopolitical Web or the Family Tree is the map, the **Wiki Page** is the actual destination. A visual graph can tell you *that* the Thieves' Guild is at war with the City Guard, but it can’t tell you *why*, it can't list the names of their secret safehouses, and it can’t store the stat-blocks of their assassins.

You absolutely still need a robust Wiki section. Here is exactly why we need these pages, what they look like in a TipTap-powered workspace, and how they bridge raw text with visual data.

---

## 🏢 1. The Faction (Organization) Wiki Page

A Faction page holds the operational truth of an organization. This is where the DM writes their history, culture, and assets, and where your TipTap auto-linking engine goes to work.

### What the Page Layout Looks Like

```
┌───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ 👥 Organizations > The Iron Syndicate                                     │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────┬───────────────────────────┤
│ [ Lore & Details ]  [ 📊 Internal Org Chart ] │ FACTION REGISTRY          │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────┤                           │
│ TITLE: The Iron Syndicate                     │ Division: Cult            │
│ MOTTO: "Gold bleeds, iron binds."             │ Base: Oakhaven Slums      │
│                                               │ Leader: Masked Handler    │
│ [TipTap Editor Area]                          ├───────────────────────────┤
│ The Syndicate began as a simple blacksmiths'   │ DIPLOMATIC STANDING       │
│ guild in the Year 400 [Timestamp]. Over time, │ 🔴 Enemy: City Guard      │
│ they expanded into smuggling...               │ 🟢 Ally: Smuggler Coalition│
│                                               ├───────────────────────────┤
│ Members like Alden [Link] use specialized...  │ WRITER STATS              │
│                                               │ Words: 840 | Reads: 3m    │
└───────────────────────────────────────────────┴───────────────────────────┘

```

### Key Components of this Wiki Page:

* **The Multi-Tab Interface:**
* **Lore & Details Tab:** A clean, uninterrupted TipTap writing workspace for history, secrets, and notes.
* **Internal Org Chart Tab:** Instead of leaving the page to look at the global map, clicking this tab flips the page over to show *only this faction's* cellular or hierarchical command structure.


* **The Metadata Sidebar Panel:** A structured card containing rigid fields (Leader, Division Type, Base of Operations).
* **The Intertwining:** Changing the "Diplomatic Standing" checkboxes in this sidebar panel is what automatically updates the lines on your global **Geopolitical Diplomacy Web**.

## 👑 2. The Family / Dynasty Wiki Page

A Family page isn't just a list of names; it's a record of bloodlines, ancestral wealth, genetic curses, and political marriages.

### What the Page Layout Looks Like

```
┌───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ 🛡️ Dynasties & Families > House of Sterling                              │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────┬───────────────────────────┤
│ [ Chronicle & History ]  [ 👑 Lineage Tree ]   │ DYNASTY REGISTRY          │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────┤                           │
│ HOUSE: House of Sterling                      │ Current Head: King Alden  │
│ COAT OF ARMS: A silver wolf on a blue field    │ Seat: Sterling Keep       │
│                                               │ Status: Ruling Family     │
│ [TipTap Editor Area]                          ├───────────────────────────┤
│ Founded during the Mythic Antiquity era, the  │ INHERITED TRAITS          │
│ Sterling bloodline carries the ancient curse  │ • Moon-touched eyes       │
│ of the Lunar Veil...                          │ • Arcane aptitude (High)  │
│                                               ├───────────────────────────┤
│ Chronicled Events:                            │ BACKLINKS RADAR           │
│ • Year 402: The Coronation of Alden [Link]    │ Mentioned in 12 pages     │
└───────────────────────────────────────────────┴───────────────────────────┘

```

### Key Components of this Wiki Page:

* **The Multi-Tab Interface:**
* **Chronicle & History Tab:** The TipTap workspace documenting their rise to power, historical betrayals, and deep lore.
* **Lineage Tree Tab:** Switches the view to the horizontal generational "Portrait Strip" layout, showing exactly who gave birth to whom, who married into the family, and who is currently dead.


* **The Inheritance Ledger:** A specialized section in the metadata sidebar for tracking heirloom items (pointing to your `Objects` wiki directory) or inherited traits.
* **The Intertwining:** When a user writes a new profile under the `Characters` wiki section and tags their family line as "House of Sterling," that character's face, status, and link automatically populate inside this page's **Lineage Tree** tab.

## 🗂️ How the Main Wiki Section Coordinates Everything

Your sidebar doesn't change into a collection of floating maps. It remains a rock-solid, highly structured directory tree.

When a DM or player navigates the wiki, they use the sidebar to open these structural text pages to read or write text. But because you have built specialized **Node View tabs** directly into the tops of these specific folder templates, the text pages can instantly transform into interactive visual graphs on demand.

This layout achieves the ultimate goal: **The wiki section remains the database of truth, while the dashboards become lens overlays that let you see how that data interacts.**
